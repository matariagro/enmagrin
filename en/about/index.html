PT. Matari Agro Indonesia

Located in Purwokerto, Central Java, we are present as a trusted partner for agricultural actors to improve the quality and productivity of the agricultural sector in Indonesia. With a focus on comprehensive consulting and training services, we assist in various aspects, from planning to agricultural production. Our commitment to sustainable agricultural development drives us to provide innovative solutions to overcome various challenges faced in the agricultural industry.

Our consulting services cover various fields, including plantations, forestry, livestock, fisheries, and marine. Our experienced team of experts is ready to assist in planning and managing agricultural projects with a targeted approach and solutions that suit the needs of clients. We understand the importance of good management in every agricultural sector, therefore, we are committed to providing comprehensive support to individuals, companies, and governments.

One of the main focuses of our consulting services is in agricultural investment planning. We assist clients in conducting market analysis, risk evaluation, and strategic planning to ensure their investments provide optimal results. We also provide consulting services in agricultural management, including natural resource management, pest and disease control, and the implementation of sustainable agricultural practices, to maintain the productivity and long-term sustainability of agricultural businesses. With our commitment to provide effective and leading solutions, PT Matari Agro Indonesia is ready to support agricultural players in achieving success and sustainable growth.

Vision

Building farmer corporations towards agricultural industrialization

Mission

  1. Educating Farmers on Sustainable Agricultural Practices
  2. Encouraging the Use of Innovative Agricultural Technologies
  3. Assisting Farmers in Diversifying Agricultural Enterprises
  4. Developing Human Resources in the Agricultural Sector
  5. Ensuring the Provision of Safe and Quality Food
  6. Supporting Innovation and Research in the Agricultural Sector
  7. Encouraging Sustainable Partnerships between Farmers and Markets

Holistic Approach

In the process of running our agricultural consulting firm and training center, we adopt a holistic approach to ensure sustainable growth for our clients. We recognize that success in agriculture involves various aspects, including technical, economic, environmental, social, and cultural aspects. To that end, we have assembled a team of professional experts consisting of agronomists, financial experts, environmental engineers, social scientists, and agricultural technology experts. With extensive knowledge and experience from various disciplines, our team can provide comprehensive and integrated solutions that address the various challenges faced by farmers and agricultural businesses.

Our agronomists provide training on best agricultural practices, while financial experts assist in designing sustainable financial plans. Environmental engineers provide consultation on water and waste management, and social scientists assist in building sustainable partnerships between farmers and local communities. Last but not least, our agricultural technology experts introduce the latest innovations that can improve efficiency and productivity. With this approach, we not only help our clients improve their agricultural yields, but also pay attention to their impact on the environment, social welfare, and economic sustainability. Together, let us join in building a brighter and more sustainable agricultural future for Indonesia.

Dr. Dyah Susanti, SP., MP.

Dyah Susanti, is an agricultural expert with a strong academic background and extensive research experience. Having completed her undergraduate and doctoral degrees at Jenderal Soedirman University (UNSOED) with a focus on plant breeding, plant science, and agricultural science, she has produced various important researches, including the assembly of superior varieties of rice with high yields and aromatic and large-seeded and early-maturing soybeans. With a dissertation title that highlights the plasticity of harvest age as an indicator of the mechanism of rice plant escape from drought stress, Dr. Dyah Susanti has demonstrated her commitment to the development of sustainable and adaptive agriculture.

As a productive researcher, Dr. Dyah Susanti has led various research projects with significant funding, including collaborations with industry and research institutions. Her work is not only limited to research but also includes scientific publications and seminar presentations, confirming her reputation as a respected expert in her field. With in-depth experience in formulating public policies and social engineering, as well as awards received for innovation and contributions to agriculture, Dr. Dyah Susanti is an ideal consultant and trainer for Matari Agro Indonesia, offering insights and practical solutions to today's agricultural challenges.
