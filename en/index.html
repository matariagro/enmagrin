Flagship Programs

  1. Agricultural Business Management Consultation

    Through this program, Matari Agro Indonesia provides consultations on agricultural business management. By focusing on financial planning, risk analysis, and marketing strategies, this program assists farmers and agricultural entrepreneurs in managing their enterprises more efficiently. In doing so, Matari Agro Indonesia supports the mission of promoting the use of innovative agricultural technologies and developing human resources in the agricultural sector.

  2. Sustainable Agriculture Training

    Matari Agro Indonesia organizes practical training programs on sustainable farming techniques. These programs are designed to enhance farmers' skills in implementing environmentally friendly and sustainable agricultural practices. By offering this training, Matari Agro Indonesia supports the vision of building sustainable agriculture in Indonesia by educating farmers on the best practices in eco-friendly farming.

  3. Agricultural Investment Planning

    Matari Agro Indonesia also offers in-depth consultation services on investment opportunities in the agricultural sector. By assisting investors and agricultural entrepreneurs in planning and managing their investments, this program contributes to the mission of making Indonesian agriculture more productive and sustainable. Thus, Matari Agro Indonesia becomes a reliable partner in advancing Indonesian agriculture towards sustainability.

Our partners include institutions, agencies, companies, universities, NGOs, and other organizations worldwide. Together, we work to achieve our shared mission of sustainability and farmer welfare.

Dr. Dyah Susanti, SP., MP.

With a dissertation title that highlights the plasticity of harvest age as an indicator of the mechanism of rice plant escape from drought stress, Dr. Dyah Susanti has demonstrated her commitment to the development of sustainable and adaptive agriculture.
